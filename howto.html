How to Use MI Symptoms

Default View

The line chart at the left should show data for cumulative COVID-like responses, and the map at the right should have the center button selected to also display cumulative COVID-like responses.

Line Chart - Interactive Features

You can toggle between seeing cumulative responses and responses over the past week.

You can also select a custom time frame by clicking on the calendar.

Use the gray slider under the line chart to fine-tune your time selection.

Toggling and adjusting the time frames will update the map accordingly!

Map - Interactive Features

Toggle amongst the three buttons under the map to view data for MI Symptoms responses. COVID-like Symptoms responses, and Proportion of COVID-like responses.

Click on any hexagon on the map to display more detailed stats about the location. Grayed-out hexagons represent areas with fewer than 20 responses.

Toggling amongst the buttons will update the line chart accordingly!
